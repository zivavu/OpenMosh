Mobile: How should the Effects Panel open?

On small screens the panel needs to be hidden by default. Pick the reveal pattern that feels best.

Bottom Sheet

Panel slides up from the bottom. A thin "handle" strip is always visible — tap to expand to full height, tap again to collapse. Common on mobile apps.

Floating Button → Full Drawer

A floating action button sits over the canvas. Tap it to open the effects panel as a full-height right-side drawer that slides in. Tap backdrop or × to close. Panel is fully hidden otherwise.

EFFECTS
CANVAS

Tab Bar

Two tabs at the top: "Canvas" and "Effects". Switching tabs swaps the full view. Simple and clear — no overlay, no drawer. Canvas is hidden while editing effects.
